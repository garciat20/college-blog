Overview

The transition to college is a crucial time full of challenges and opportunities. For many students, the financial burden of tuition, textbooks, and living expenses is grand, often requiring to take on multiple jobs, taking on significant student loan debt, or both. Not just financially, emotional and academic pressures are always present as students go through new relationships and the daunting task of coursework. Despite these challenges, going to a university offers a unique environment for personal and intellectual growth, offers opportunities to explore new interests, meet different people, and develop critical skills for the future.
